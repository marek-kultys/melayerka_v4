Bio

Mela graduated in 2011 from Central Saint Martins College of Art & Design in London, having previously studied at the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw, Poland. Mela is the winner of the Cass Art Painting Prize 2011. Polish born, she now lives and works in east London. Mela exhibits internationally.

Mela is represented by Maria Stenfors, London.
www.mariastenfors.com
